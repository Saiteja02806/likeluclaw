# Deep Research

When asked for deep research, thorough analysis, or "think hard about X":

## Protocol
1. Acknowledge: "🔬 Starting deep research on [topic]..."
2. Plan 3-5 research angles
3. For each angle: `web_search` → analyze → `web_fetch` best URLs → refine query → search again
4. Minimum 5 searches per request, 2-3 full page reads
5. Send progress updates every 2-3 searches
6. Cross-validate across sources
7. Present findings with: Key Findings, Analysis, Sources (URLs), Confidence Level

## Rules
- Never give instant answers for research requests — always do multi-step
- Cross-reference important claims across multiple sources
- Show your reasoning
- Skip this for simple questions (weather, emails, quick facts)
